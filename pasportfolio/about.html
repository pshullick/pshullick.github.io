About Me

Patricia Shullick, 21

Graphic Design | Photography | Web Design

Hi There! Thanks for visiting my portfolio site! I am currently in my third year here at Mercyhurst. I've found a passion for graphic design in every aspect. I'm not sure which area of design I want to work in after graduation, but that leaves several options! I work in the adobe suite proficiently. I have experience with video and photo editing. I work with the art department here, as well as with the activities council. Outside of design, I have worked in retail and at the coffee bar on campus. I am also the chair person for the orientation leaders here at Mercyhurst, also known as the Laker Leaders. I enjoy integrating my knowledge fromt these experiences into my graphics and vice versa, after all, design is all around us.
