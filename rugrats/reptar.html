Reptar

The baby's favorite dino


Reptar is a fictional character from the American animated television series Rugrats. Reptar is a green T. rex (and sometimes appears in red-violet and lilac) with rounded, blue spike-like appendages on his back, which intentionally causes him to resemble and spoof Godzilla.
